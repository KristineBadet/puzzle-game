The Book Borrowing Mystery

Three friends—Alex, Brian, and Charlie—each borrowed a different book (one was Mystery, one was Fantasy, and one was Science Fiction) from the library on different days of the week (Monday, Wednesday, and Friday).

Using the clues below, can you determine who borrowed which book and on what day?

Clues:

1. Alex did not borrow the book on Monday.

2. The person who borrowed the Fantasy book did so on Friday.

3. Charlie borrowed the book on Wednesday.

4. Brian did not borrow the Science Fiction book.
