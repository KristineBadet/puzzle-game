The Case of the Missing Diamond

A priceless diamond was stolen from a museum exhibit. The security footage showed that the power went out for exactly 10 seconds, during which the diamond disappeared.

The police questioned three suspects, all of whom were inside the museum at the time:

Alice (The Journalist): "I was recording the exhibit for my news report. When the power went out, I heard footsteps but couldn't see anything."

Brian (The Guard): "I was in the security room. I saw the cameras go black, and when the power returned, the diamond was gone!"

Charlie (The Visitor): "I was using my phone to take a picture of the diamond. The power outage ruined my shot, so I had to retake it later."

The detective immediately knew who was lying.
